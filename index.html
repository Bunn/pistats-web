Features

Everything you need to monitor and control your Pi-hole, beautifully designed for Apple platforms.

Real-time Monitoring

Watch your Pi-hole statistics update live with beautiful charts and metrics that give you instant insights.

Quick Enable/Disable

Toggle your Pi-hole protection on and off with a single tap. Perfect for temporary access to blocked content.

iOS Widgets

Add widgets to your home screen for at-a-glance statistics. See blocked queries and protection status instantly.

Menu Bar App

Access Pi Stats directly from your Mac menu bar. Quick, convenient, and always just a click away.

Secure Storage

Your credentials are safely stored in the system Keychain. Your authentication tokens never leave your device.

Multiple Pi-holes

Monitor multiple Pi-hole instances from a single app. Perfect for complex network setups and redundancy.

Enhanced with Pi Monitor

For optional monitoring of your Raspberry Pi, install Pi Monitor for enhanced performance insights and real-time statistics about your Pi's CPU, memory, and temperature.
